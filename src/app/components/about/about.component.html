About Me

“When yoga and I first met in 2015 via a lovely friend of mine, I have to admit that I did’t like it. After a couple of months, when my dear friend got her certificate and started teaching yoga, I unwillingly accepted her request and started attending her class to support her. Incredibly, I realized that all the emotions which had disturbed me when I did yoga for the first time, started changing day by day, and yoga started healing my body and my soul. I could manage to calm my mind which had never been quiet at all and aches and pains gave way to relaxing.”

Firdevs was born in Turkey in 1986. She grew up in a lovely city called Bodrum. She went to Istanbul University in 2004 and she received her bachelor of science in geological engineering. After graduation, she worked as an engineering geologist for years. She started doing yoga via a friend of hers, who is a geologist too, in 2015. In time, she realized the effects of yoga on her body and her soul and she started doing yoga practice daily, learning and trying every kind of yoga. She moved to Copenhagen with her love in 2017. Here, when she took time off from yoga practice, her anxiety disorder came back again, and she started to struggle with insomnia, pains in her lower back and neck, and migraine attacks. While she was searching what could be the more beneficial solutions instead of symptomatic drugs, she found again meditation and yoga. Thus, her own yoga journey started again and this time she wanted to help other people too. She wants to show the healing power of yoga and meditation to everyone. She completed 200 hours of Teacher Training with Zeynep Aksoy, and got her RYT200 certificate. Moreover, she is continuing her training with the 1000 hours of Teacher Training with Yogarama.
